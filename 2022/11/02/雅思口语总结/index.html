雅思口语总结

举例子/细说

  • like
  • for example
  • for instance
  • to be more specific
  • in particular
  • especially

原因

  • because
  • since
  • the reason is that ..
  • the thing is ..
  • because of the fact that ..
  • due to the fact that ..

首先（或用于开头）

  • first of all
  • to begin with
  • the first thing that i usually do is ..
  • the most obvious/important thing that i want to say is that ..

其次（或用于补充）

  • besides
  • what’s more
  • apart from that
  • also
  • in addition
  • another thing that i usually do is ..
  • .. not only .. but also ..
  • .. either .. or ..

喜欢

  • i am fond of ..
  • be keen on ..
  • be into ..
  • be big on ..
  • be a fan of
  • be crazy about ..
  • i can’t stand
  • every time when .. , i always ..
  • as long as i have some spare time, i will always ..
  • no matter when .. , i always/usually ..
  • whenever .. , …
  • it is no exaggeration to say that + as long as i .. , i always ..

不喜欢

  • i have to say that ..
  • i have never .. for years/ages/since..
  • i remember that .. , but .. from then on, i hate ..

好处

  • it is a brilliant/splendid/wonderful/perfect/good/essential way for me to ..

坏处

  • i have to admit/say/mention/emphasis that + it is a good way for me to ..

观点

  • in my view
  • from my perspective
  • in my opinion
  • as far as i am concerned
  • from my point of view
  • i think/guess/suppose/believe
  • well, to be honest, it doesn’t have a certain answer
  • i can’t give you a certain answer
  • i am not sure

过去类

注意时态：过去时、现在时、完成时

  • i can remember clearly that ..
  • well, it has been a long time. let me see
  • if my memory goes right, I probably/possibly ate/had ..
  • it seems like that ..
  • it is likely that ..

未来类

  • may - might
  • can - could
  • will - would

估计

  • it is very likely that ..
  • it seems that ..
  • there is a big chance that ..
