整体写作思路（5个核心审题步骤）

1. 区分动静

2. 数据含义

3. 主体段的分段思路

  • 动静结合（body 1: 对比初始值大小 body2: 对比变化）
  • 合并同类项（数值大小、趋势方向、变化幅度、数据类型）

4. 提取主要特征

5. overview

  • 一句话总结2个最核心的主要特征+linker(and/while)

6个功能句型

1. 改写题目

  • 题目给出的形式：主谓宾The chart/graph/table below shows +宾语+地点+时间

  • 改写方法：

    • The chart/graph/table不改
    • below去掉
    • show=illustrate/give information about (describe/demonstrate/depict/tell都不合适）

/ 注意：一般现在时/主谓一致 /

  • 宾语是改写重点（数据含义）
    • 只换词（词性、近义词、解释、增加修饰）
    • 换结构(名词性短语和名词性从句的替换）
    • 精准数据含义

例子：

where the money came from（原句）

where the operational fund came from

the source of money

the amount of money from three different sources

例子：

how it was distributed（原句）

how it was allocated/spent

money spent on different areas/categories

the proportion/percentage of money spent on different categories

  • 微调地点和时间

例子：

for 2017 and 2018（原句）

in 2017 and 2018

例子：

in one area of Britain（原句）

in a particular/certain region in the UK

  • 动态图小作文的万能改法

    • the graph illustrates how + 宾语 + had changed + 地点 + 时间.

  • 静态图的万能改法

    • The graph illustrates how + 宾语 + varied + between/among.. + 地点 + 时间.

2. 极值句型

  • 定性（主要特征）+ 定量（数据支持）

    • 图例作主语

      例子：

      The national government was the biggest source of police budget.

      The national government contributes most money to the police force.

    • “最”作主语

      例子：

      The primary/most important source of police budget was the national government.

      The funding for police mainly came from the national government.

  • 引出数据的方式

    • 三种复合句

      • 连词(and/because/as/although)

        例子: The most important source of police budget was the national government, and it gave175.5m pounds in 2017.

        • 定语从句

          例子：The most important source of police budget was the national government， which/who gave 175.5m pounds in 2017.

        • 伴随状语(with + 名词; 非谓语 doing/done)

          例子：

          The national government was the biggest source of police budget, contributing 175.5m pounds in 2017.

          The funding for police mainly came from the national government, with 175.5m pounds coming for this source.

          Salaries were the biggest cost, with money spent on them accounting for 75% in 2017.(with 75% of money spent on this category)

          Money spent on salaries was the most, accounting for 75% in 2017.

    • 简单的引出数据的方式

      • at 引出

        例子：

        The percentage of money spent on salaries was the highest, at 75% in 2017.

      • 利用括号表明不重点说明的数据

        例子：

        Spending on building and transport(8%) was over two times that on technology(17%) in this year.

3. 占比句型（3种）

例子：

75% of money was spent on salaries in 2017.

Spending on salaries accounted for 75% in 2017.
(注意：occupy/take up都不能用；可以用make up)

The percentage of money spent on salaries was 75% in 2017.

4. 趋势句型（5个元素：主体、趋势词、幅度词、数据、时间）

1. 主体句型

  • 简单句

    • 主谓

      例子：

      Money from the national government increased from …in 2017 to …in the next year.

    • there be

      例子：

      There was an increase of… in money from the national government in the two-year period.

  • 主谓宾

    例子：

    The period witnessed/recorded a rise of…in money from the national government.

  • 连词复合句

    • 趋势句型1，and then +趋势句型2

      例子：

      There was some fluctuation between 20,000 and around 30, 000 in visitors going to mountains in the first 12 years, and then the number of visitors went up gradually/steadily to about 35, 000 in 2007.

  • 定从复合句

    • 趋势句型1，after which +趋势句型2

      例子：

      There was some fluctuation between 20,000 and around 30, 000 in visitors going to mountains in the first 12 years, after which the number of visitors went up gradually/steadily to about 35, 000 in 2007.

  • 伴随状语

    • 趋势句型1，followed by +a drop/decrease to/of+data

      例子：

      There was some fluctuation between 20,000 and around 30, 000 in visitors going to mountains in the first 12 years, followed by a gradual increase to about 35, 000 in 2007.

    • 趋势句型1，before+a drop/decrease to/of+data

      例子：

      There was some fluctuation between 20,000 and around 30, 000 in visitors going to mountains in the first 12 years, before a gradual increase to about 35, 000 in 2007.

  • 补充（预测的表达）

    • 错误用法：不建议用will，因为will表示100%会发生

      错误例子：

      The figure will increase to 45,000 in 2027.

    • 正确用法：

      be predicted/projected/estimated/expected to increase to…

      正确例子：

      A increased slightly at first, peaking at 45% in 2004, after which the figure decreased to…

2. 趋势词

  • 上升
    • 动词：increase/rise(rose)/grow(grew)/go up(went up)
    • 名词：an increase/a rise/a growth
  • 下降
    • 动词：decrease/drop(dropped)/decline/fall(fell)/go down
    • 名词：a decrease/a drop/a decline/a fall
  • 保持平稳
    • 动词：keep/maintain/remain/stay + stable/steady/the same/unchanged
  • 波动
    • 动词：fluctuate
    • 名词：some/a lot of fluctuation

3. 幅度词

  • 显著/大/快
    • 形容词：significant/remarkable/sharp/considerable/dramatic
    • 副词：dramatically
  • 轻微
    • 形容词：slight/mild/moderate
    • 副词：slightly

4. 数据

  • 引出数据（介词的用法）
    • 从X上升到Y：increase from X to Y
    • 上升到Y：increased to Y
    • 上升了Z：increase by Z 或者 an increase of Z
    • 在X达到了峰值：reach a peak at X 或者 peaked at X
  • 大约的表达：about/approximately/around/nearly/roughly + 数据

5. 时间状语

例子：

from 2017 to 2018

during/in/over the (given) two-year period

between… and …

例子：

in 2017

initially

例子：

in 2018

at the end of the period

  • 特殊的时间间隔：in the first/next/following/final decade(s)

5.对比句型

1. 形容词

  • 最高级

    例子：

    South Africa is the biggest coffee producer, contributing to 40% of global production.

  • 比较级

    例子：

    This is followed by Indonesia, whose production accounted for 20%, 5% and 7% higher/more than that of Japan and Vietnam.

  • 其它

    similar, the same, an opposite pattern/trend

2. 动词

  • overtake（万能）

  • outnumber

  • outperform

    例子：

    The boys liking to play football outnumbered girls.

    The number of boys liking to play football overtook that of girls.

3. 副词

  • By contrast

  • In contrast

  • By comparison

  • On the other hand

  • (以上通常用在body2的段首）

    例子：

    South Africa is the biggest coffee producer, contributing to 40% of global production. Another three major coffee producers are IN, JP, VT, where 13-20% of coffee was produced. By contrast, the other countries produce only 12% of coffee in total(=totally/collectively).

4. 介词短语、非谓语（作状语）

  • followed by

    例子：

    South Africa is the biggest coffee producer, contributing to 40% of global production. This is followed by Indonesia, whose production accounted for 20%, 5% and 7% higher/more than that of Japan and Vietnam.

  • compared with+ 数据+介词+对比对象

    例子：

    Most profit goes to delivery at 55%, compared with 10% each to exporters and producers.

5. 连词while

  • 连词while是连词，不能引导单独的句子

    例子：

    Most profit goes to delivery at 55%, while exporters and producers only make 10% of money each (respectively).

6. 倍数/分数

例子：

The consumption of coffee in Europe is five times more than that of Japan.

five times more than = five times = five times as much as

6.overview

用法

  • Overall, it is obvious/evident that…+2key features(中间加连接词）

  • key feature 1: 趋势（如：大多数都上升，但程度不同）

  • key feature 2: 极大值

  • 常用的句式：

    1. show an upward trend/a downward trend(不能替换成tendency)
    2. albeit to varying degrees —— 但程度不同
